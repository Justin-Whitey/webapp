Hamster

Global Population
There are approximately 20 species of hamsters globally, with only five commonly domesticated as pets. While exact global population figures are unavailable, wild hamsters inhabit regions across Europe, the Middle East, and Central and East Asia.

Rabbit

Global Population
Domesticated Rabbits: Approximately 709 million worldwide.
Wild Rabbits: Difficult to estimate, but populations are in the hundreds of millions globally.

Top 3 Rare Rabbit Breeds
Sumatran Striped Rabbit (Nesolagus netscheri): Elusive and rare, found only in the forests of Sumatra.

Riverine Rabbit (Bunolagus monticularis): Critically endangered in South Africa due to habitat loss.

Columbia Basin Pygmy Rabbit: Once thought extinct, this small rabbit has become a focus of conservation in the U.S.
